New Delhi

In the city centre and near a metro station, Le Meridien New Delhi is in an area with good shopping. Notable landmarks in the area include Jantar Mantar and India Gate. Worlds of Wonder and KidZania Delhi NCR are also worth visiting. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including outlet shopping.

About this property

Le Meridien New Delhi

This hotel features a full-service spa, an outdoor pool and a restaurant. Free WiFi in public areas and free valet parking are also provided. Additionally, a fitness centre, a bar/lounge and a snack bar/deli are on-site. All 358 soundproofed rooms provide free WiFi, free wired Internet and 24-hour room service. Guests can enjoy deep soaking bathtubs, and other standard amenities include sitting areas, minibars and LCD TVs.
